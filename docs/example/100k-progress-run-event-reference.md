## Event Overview

The 100K Progress Run by HelloRun is an accumulated virtual run challenge where participants complete a total distance of 100 kilometres within the official event period.

You do not need to finish 100 km in one activity.

You can complete your distance through multiple runs, jogs, walks, trail runs, or hikes from May 13 to May 31, 2026. Each activity must be recorded using a fitness tracking app and submitted through the HelloRun platform for review.

This challenge is designed for runners, walkers, beginners, fitness enthusiasts, students, professionals, and community groups who want to build consistency through progress-based participation.

The goal is simple.

Complete 100 km before the challenge ends.

## Participation Mechanics

1. Register for the 100K Progress Run by HelloRun.
2. Complete your activities anytime from May 13 to May 31, 2026.
3. Record each activity using a fitness tracking app.
4. Submit your activity proof through the HelloRun platform.
5. Wait for your submission to be reviewed.
6. Continue submitting activities until your approved total reaches at least 100 km.
7. Once your approved total reaches 100 km, you will be marked as a finisher.
8. Finishers will receive official event recognition after the review period.

---

## Distance Requirement

Participants must complete at least 100 km in total.

The distance may be completed through multiple activities.

Example:

| Activity | Distance |
|---|---:|
| Run 1 | 5.00 km |
| Run 2 | 8.50 km |
| Walk 1 | 4.00 km |
| Trail Run 1 | 12.30 km |
| Run 3 | 10.00 km |
| Continued Submissions | Until total reaches 100 km |

---

## Minimum Distance Per Submission

Each submitted activity must be at least 1 km.

Activities below 1 km will not be counted toward the official total.

This helps keep the review process clean and prevents accidental or incomplete activities from being submitted.

---

## Completion Rule

A participant becomes an official finisher once their approved activity submissions reach at least 100 km.

Examples:

| Approved Total | Status |
|---:|---|
| 87.50 km | In Progress |
| 99.90 km | Not Yet Complete |
| 100.00 km | Complete |
| 105.25 km | Complete |

Extra distance may still appear in the participant’s total progress, depending on the leaderboard format.

## Accepted Proof of Activity

Participants must submit a clear screenshot from a fitness tracking app.

The screenshot should show:

- Distance completed
- Activity date
- Duration or moving time
- Activity type
- Fitness app source
- Route or activity summary, if available
- Pace, if available
- Participant name or profile name, if available

## Submission Rules

- Activities must be completed from May 13 to May 31, 2026.
- Each activity must be submitted through the HelloRun platform.
- Each submission must include a clear screenshot of the recorded activity.
- The activity distance must be visible.
- The activity date must be visible.
- The same activity cannot be submitted more than once.
- Edited or manipulated screenshots may be rejected.
- Unclear screenshots may be returned for correction or rejected.
- Pending submissions do not count toward official progress until approved.
- Rejected submissions will not count toward the 100 km total.
- Submissions must be sent on or before May 31, 2026 at 11:59 PM.

---

## Progress Tracking

Participant progress will be based on approved submissions only.

Example:

| Submission | Distance | Status | Counted Progress |
|---|---:|---|---:|
| Activity 1 | 5.20 km | Approved | 5.20 km |
| Activity 2 | 8.00 km | Approved | 13.20 km |
| Activity 3 | 10.50 km | Pending | 13.20 km |
| Activity 4 | 6.30 km | Approved | 19.50 km |
| Activity 5 | 3.00 km | Rejected | 19.50 km |

Pending activities are still under review.

Only approved activities increase the official progress total.

## Leaderboard and Ranking

The main recognition for this event is completion-based.

All participants who reach at least 100 km through approved submissions will be recognised as official finishers.

Optional leaderboard categories may include:

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| 100K Finishers | Participants who completed at least 100 km |
| Top Distance Finishers | Participants with the highest approved total distance |
| Early Finishers | Participants who reached 100 km earliest |
| Most Consistent Participants | Participants with regular approved submissions across the event period |

Recommended format:

Use 100K Finishers as the main leaderboard.

Use Top Distance Finishers as a secondary leaderboard only.

This keeps the event encouraging while still giving competitive runners a reason to push further.

## Participant Reminders

- Record every activity using a fitness app.
- Make sure the date and distance are visible in your screenshot.
- Submit activities as soon as possible after completing them.
- Check your progress regularly.
- Avoid duplicate submissions.
- Use safe routes.
- Stay hydrated.
- Follow local road and trail safety rules.
- Do not force yourself to continue if you feel unwell.
- Give yourself enough rest between activities.

## Safety Guidelines

Participants are responsible for their own safety during the challenge.

Before running, walking, hiking, or doing trail activities:

- Choose a safe route.
- Run during safe hours when possible.
- Bring water for longer activities.
- Wear proper footwear.
- Follow traffic rules.
- Inform someone if you are running alone.
- Avoid unsafe weather conditions.
- Rest when needed.

---

## Disqualification or Rejection Grounds

A submission may be rejected if:

- The screenshot is unclear.
- The distance is not visible.
- The date is not visible.
- The activity was completed outside the challenge period.
- The activity type is not accepted.
- The same activity was submitted more than once.
- The screenshot appears edited or manipulated.
- The activity appears to be from a vehicle or non-foot-based movement.
- The participant submits false or misleading activity information.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to finish 100 km in one day?

No.

This is an accumulated challenge. You can complete 100 km through multiple activities from May 13 to May 31, 2026.

### Can I walk instead of run?

Yes.

Walking is accepted as long as the activity is properly recorded and submitted.

### Can hiking count?

Yes.

Hiking is accepted if it is recorded through a fitness app and the distance is clearly shown.

### Can I submit more than one activity?

Yes.

You can submit multiple activities until your approved total reaches 100 km or more.

### What happens if I only complete 80 km?

You will not be marked as an official 100K finisher, but your approved progress can still be recorded as participation progress.

### Can I submit an activity done before May 13?

No.

Only activities completed from May 13 to May 31, 2026 will count.

### Can I submit after May 31?

No.

The final submission deadline is May 31, 2026 at 11:59 PM.

### Do pending submissions count?

No.

Only approved submissions count toward the official total.

### What if my submission is rejected?

You may submit another valid activity as long as the submission deadline has not passed.

### Can extra distance above 100 km count?

Yes.

Extra approved distance may appear in your total progress and may be used for optional leaderboard recognition.
