# HelloRun Theme

HelloRun is about movement, momentum, and shared progress.
The icon shows a runner stepping forward inside a conversation shape.
That tells you this platform is not only about finishing a run, but about staying connected, motivated, and visible as you move.
The warm runner signals energy and action.
The cool background signals trust, clarity, and community.
Together, they frame HelloRun as a friendly, social space where running is simple, inclusive, and goal driven.

You are not just tracking distance.
You are joining a conversation around consistency, health, and achievement.

## Possible Landing Page Content

### Hero section
Run anywhere. Show up with purpose.
Discover events that match your pace, submit completed runs, and stay connected to a community that keeps moving.

### Hero proof points
- Active events
- Approved finishes

### What HelloRun does
- Find virtual events that fit your pace
- Submit results online without app or GPS lock-in
- Keep finishes, rankings, and recognition in one place

### Why HelloRun
You run on your own schedule.
You still feel part of something bigger.
HelloRun keeps it simple so you focus on moving, not managing tools.

### For runners
Find events that match your pace.
Submit finishes online.

### For organisers
Create event pages quickly.

### Call to action

Sign up free.
Move. Log. Finish.
